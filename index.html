🎵 Audio QR Generator

Create QR codes for your Google Drive audio files

⚠️ Important: Set File Permissions First

  1. In Google Drive, right-click your audio file
  2. Click Share
  3. Under "General access", select "Anyone with the link"
  4. Set role to Viewer
  5. Click Done

Without this step, students won't be able to play the audio!

How to use:

  1. Paste up to 5 Google Drive audio links below
  2. Click "Generate QR Code"
  3. Save or print the QR code
  4. Share with students - they scan to listen!
