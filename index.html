Create QR codes for your Dropbox audio files

📁 How to Get Dropbox Links

  1. Upload your audio file to Dropbox
  2. Click Share on the file
  3. Click "Create link"
  4. Click "Copy link"
  5. Paste the link below

Dropbox links work best! Make sure the link sharing is enabled.

How to use:

  1. Paste up to 5 Dropbox audio links below
  2. Click "Generate QR Code"
  3. Save or print the QR code
  4. Share with students - they scan to listen!
